Basic Foundation Course

New to Djembe? New to a Rhythm instrument?

This is your place to start. Enter the training and, in an easy-to-start course, learn how to play the Djembe and navigate through the world of Indian Percussion with Taufiq Qureshi. Get it right — At The Foundation Room.

Course Highlights

  • Designed for anybody who wishes to start playing a rhythm instrument
  • Train on any instrument — Djembe, Cajon, Darbuka, Duff
  • Start training without an instrument using specified household items
  • A 4-Level foundation course from Level A to Level D
  • Introduction to basic playing and popular world grooves
  • Indian rhythm concepts (Taal, Bols, Kayda, Tihai, Gharana & Tukda)
  • Step-by-step detailed guidance
  • Access at your own time
  • Open to all age groups
  • Be part of a worldwide rhythm community
  • Get monitored and receive feedback
  • Be part of performance training, showcases and progress tests
  • Be eligible for the exclusive Live trainings with the Master Taufiq Qureshi himself
  • In-class support with expert trainers
  • Experience and bring an environment of Music to your home
  • Train at TFR PL>Y and then practice what you've learnt thoroughly in the Practice Room

Intermediate Course

Already playing?

For those who wish to pick up further concepts and details of Indian rhythm and the tabla repertoire on the Djembe.

Course Highlights

  • Designed to hone further skills on the instrument
  • Develop more control, musicality, application and performance confidence
  • 4-Level Training Course from Intermediate A to Intermediate D
  • Cover further applications to popular world grooves
  • Cover more detailed study of the Tabla Repertoire (Punjab, Delhi, Ajrada Gharana, making Tihai's, intro to Peshkar and more)
